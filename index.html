Nigeria

My city

Nigeria is known for its oil production, business spirit, and cuisine. It is the residence of some of Africa's wealthiest men. In addition, its cultural richness, natural packs, islands, beaches, cuisines, and diversity make it a unique destination to explore.

Japan

Island country

Japan is a fascinating country with a long and rich history, filled with unique and interesting cultural customs. From its rich culinary culture to its breathtaking natural beauty, there is something for everyone to enjoy in Japan. With so much to see and do, it's no wonder why Japan is so well known around the world.

Peru

richest country

Peru is one of the richest countries in the world in terms of natural resources. Gold, silver, copper, zinc, lead, and iron are found across the country, and there are reserves of oil and natural gas. Even so, jobs there can be very hard to find, and Peru remains one of the world's poorest countries.
